## Brand & Visual Identity

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- **Lexecon Blue:** `#1E3A8A` (Trust, security, technology)
- **Cryptographic Gold:** `#F59E0B` (Premium, value, cryptography)
- **Success Green:** `#10B981` (Growth, validation)
- **Alert Red:** `#EF4444` (Risk, urgency)

**Neutral Palette:**
- **Dark:** `#1F2937` (Text, headers)
- **Gray:** `#6B7280` (Body text)
- **Light Gray:** `#F3F4F6` (Backgrounds)
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` (Clean space)

### Typography
- **Headers:** Montserrat Bold (32-48pt)
- **Subheaders:** Montserrat SemiBold (24-28pt)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (14-18pt)
- **Code/Technical:** JetBrains Mono (12-14pt)

- Padlock (security)
- Chain link (blockchain concept)
- Shield (protection)
- Checkmark (governance approval)

## Presentation Tips

### Before the Meeting
- [ ] Rehearse 5+ times
- [ ] Time yourself (15-20 minutes for full deck)
- [ ] Prepare for questions
- [ ] Test screen sharing
- [ ] Have backup copies (USB, cloud, email)

### During the Presentation
- **Pace:** 1-2 minutes per slide
- **Eye contact:** Look at camera (virtual) or audience
- **Energy:** Show passion for the problem/solution
- **Questions:** Invite them throughout or save for end

### After the Presentation
- [ ] Send thank-you email (within 24 hours)
- [ ] Include PDF of deck
- [ ] Offer additional materials (whitepaper, demo)
- [ ] Schedule follow-up call
- [ ] Add to CRM/tracking system

## Customization Notes

### For Different Audiences

**VCs (Tech-focused):**
- Emphasize: Technical moat, open-source strategy, developer traction
- Show: GitHub metrics, code quality, architectural diagrams

**Strategic Investors (Enterprise):**
- Emphasize: Enterprise sales, compliance value, ROI
- Show: Customer logos, case studies, unit economics

**Angel Investors (High-level):**
- Emphasize: Market opportunity, team, vision
- Keep: High-level, less technical detail

### Length Variations
- **Quick Pitch (5 slides):** 1, 2, 3, 4, 18
- **Executive Summary (10 slides):** 1-4, 7, 8, 10, 14, 18, 20
- **Full Deck (20 slides):** All slides
- **Demo Day (3 minutes):** Slides 1-4 only
